Blinkit Data analysis

Project Title: Data Analysis for Blinkit


Customer Behavior and Operational Insights Project Overview: The goal of this project is to analyze customer data from Blinkit (formerly Grofers) to uncover insights related to customer behavior, purchasing patterns, and operational efficiency. Using data-driven techniques, the project aims to provide actionable insights for improving customer experience, inventory management, and delivery logistics.
Key Objectives: Customer Segmentation: Identify different customer segments based on their purchasing habits, frequency, and transaction history.
Sales Trend Analysis: Analyze sales data over time to identify seasonal trends, popular product categories, and the impact of promotions or discounts.
Purchase Frequency & Lifetime Value (CLV): Determine the average customer lifetime value and how often customers make repeat purchases.
Product Recommendation: Leverage past purchasing data to suggest related products to customers and improve the recommendation engine.
Delivery Performance: Analyze delivery times, order fulfillment, and identify bottlenecks in the supply chain.
Churn Analysis: Identify factors that contribute to customer churn and recommend strategies for retention.
Methodology:Data collection and preprocessing from Blinkit’s order and customer database.
Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) to identify patterns, anomalies, and insights.
Use of machine learning techniques (e.g., clustering, regression models) to make predictions and segment customers.
Visualization of key metrics using tools like Tableau or Power BI for clear reporting.
Tools & Technologies:
Programming: Python (pandas, numpy, matplotlib, seaborn)
Data Analysis & Modeling: SQL, Scikit-learn
Data Visualization: Tableau, Power BI
Data Collection: API integration (if applicable)
Expected Outcome:
Clear understanding of customer behavior and market demand.
Actionable insights to improve operational strategies, including inventory, delivery times, and marketing.
Improved customer retention and experience based on data-driven decisions.
